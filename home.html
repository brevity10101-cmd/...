Key Features

AI Flashcard Generation

Automatically generate flashcards from uploaded notes or lesson summaries. Organize them into decks for different subjects.

Quiz Generation

Generate quizzes based on selected topics or flashcards. Receive instant feedback and explanations.

Study Group Collaboration

Share flashcard decks or notes with friends and study groups. Collaborate in real-time.

Educational Trackers & Goals

Track progress with visual feedback, set study time goals, and complete short note targets.

Handwritten Notes to Summary

Upload documents or images of handwritten notes and get them summarized in seconds.

Recommended Study Plans

Get optimized study plans based on your educational grade and individual needs.

About Us

Brevity is developed by Master Minders, a dedicated team of students from Holy Cross College Payagala. Our mission is to transform the learning experience for students preparing for critical exams like Grade 10, Grade 11, and O/L levels.

We combine advanced AI technologies with user-friendly features to make learning smarter, faster, and more personalized.
